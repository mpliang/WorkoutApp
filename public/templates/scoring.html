Scoring is completed automatically.

Simply log your workouts on the log exercise page.

Points will be converted based on activity into a points per hour (PPH) format.

High Intensity Exercise: 7000 PPH

This is meant to encapsulate things that are anerobic exercises, like:

  1. Weight lifting.
  2. Circuit training.
  3. Crossfit.
  4. Spin class.
  5. Sprints.

Low Intensity Exercise: 5000 PPH

This is meant to encapsulate things that are aerobic, and slow paced, like:

  1. Jogging.
  2. Swimming.
  3. Yoga.

General Sporting: 3500 PPH

This is meant to include any sport that has a high mix of both high and low intensity work, like:

  1. Basketball.
  2. Soccer.
  3. Ultimate Frisbee.
