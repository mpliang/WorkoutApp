What?

GitFit is an outlit for:

  1. Tracking workouts.
  2. Exercise variety: different workouts are worth different points.
  3. Building motivation: at the end of each work week points will be compared.
  4. Bonding: the office can decide beforehand what the reward will be.
  5. Most importantly: having fun!

Why?

Exercise is scientifically shown to:

  1. Increase your immune system.
  2. Improve your sleep.
  3. Balance your emotions.
  4. Create stronger bonds between friends and colleagues if done together.

Matches

Each week you will be matched against another player with guidelines as follows:

  1. The week is considered Monday through Friday.
  2. You will be expected to track your own workouts on the log exercise page.
  3. Points will be calculated automatically.
  4. Your total points will be updated on the weekly matchup and scoreboard pages.
