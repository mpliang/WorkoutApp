Why?

Exercise is scientifically shown to:

increase your immune system,

improve your sleep,

balance your emotions,

and

create stronger bonds between friends and colleagues if done together.

Match Ups

Each week you will be pitted against another player on the weekly matchup page.
The week is considered Monday through Sunday.
Record your workouts on the log exercise page. Points will be calculated automatically and your total points will be updated on the weekly matchup and scoreboard pages.

Rewards/Compensations/Penalties

There are no prizes or punishments for participating in this league. I don’t feel like handling people’s money for this, and do I want to tempt people to cheat since this experiment relies entirely on good sportsmanship. I have no idea how I would enforce any punishments anyhow.
